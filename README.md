# 🗺️ Historical GeoJSON Maps for After Effects (GEOlayers 3) & GIS

Welcome to my repository of unique historical vector boundaries! I reconstruct all these maps manually using QGIS, based on archives, de-jure constitutional borders, and real geographic coordinates (WGS 84 / EPSG:4326).

All files are optimized: pixelated edges are smoothed, internal holes are removed, and borders are buffered for perfect rendering in After Effects at any camera zoom level.

### 🛠️ Guide: How to Use These Maps in After Effects (GEOlayers 3)
1. Right-click the **"Download File"** link in the catalog above and choose **"Save link as..."**. Save it with the `.geojson` extension.
2. Open After Effects and launch the **GEOlayers 3** plugin panel.
3. On the plugin panel, click the menu button (three lines) $\rightarrow$ **Import GeoJSON** and select your downloaded file.
4. The shape will instantly appear on your Mercator map right in its correct historical place and automatically link to the GEOlayers camera!

### 🛠️ Инструкция: Как использовать карту в After Effects (GeoLayers 3)
1. Нажмите на ссылку **«Скачать файл»** в таблице выше правой кнопкой мыши и выберите **«Сохранить ссылку как...»** (Save link as...). Сохраните файл с расширением `.geojson`.
2. Откройте After Effects и запустите панель плагина **GEOlayers 3**.
3. На панели плагина нажмите кнопку меню $\rightarrow$ **Import GeoJSON** и выберите скачанный файл.
